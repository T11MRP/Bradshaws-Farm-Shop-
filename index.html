About Us

Rooted in Perton.
Loved by Locals.

For over 35 years, Bradshaws has been the beating heart of Perton's community. We're an independent farm shop stocking the freshest fruit and vegetables, quality meats, artisan produce and local bakery goods — all under the warm wooden roof that our customers know and love.

We care deeply about what we stock and where it comes from. Walk in and you'll immediately feel the difference — this is shopping as it should be.

"The friendliest shop in Perton — always fresh, always welcoming."

What We Stock

A Proper Shop,
Full of Good Things

From rainbow carrots pulled that morning to award-winning artisan preserves, homemade pies and locally baked breads — there is always something worth discovering at Bradshaws.

Fruit & Vegetables

Sourced fresh every day. From seasonal British favourites to exotic fruits and heritage vegetables — colourful, vibrant and always at their best.

Butchery & Pies

Artisan Bakery

Throughout the Year

Seasons Worth
Looking Forward To

Each season brings something special. From strawberry picking on a warm summer morning to choosing your perfect pumpkin in October — these are the moments that bring Perton families back year after year.

Summer

Strawberry
Picking

Pick your own sun-warmed strawberries straight from the patch. A beloved Bradshaws tradition — the taste of a proper British summer, picked by you.

Autumn · October

Pumpkin
Picking

Our famous October pumpkin picking event. Classic orange, white ghost, warty and mini varieties — a highlight of the Perton calendar every single year.

November – December

Christmas Trees
& Festive Produce

Real Christmas trees arriving each November, alongside beautiful wreaths, seasonal fruit and veg, and everything needed for a proper family Christmas from a shop that genuinely cares. It's the most magical time of year at Bradshaws.

Winter

Cosy Season
Treats

Firewood bags, warming pantry staples, festive bakes and everything that makes staying in feel special when the nights draw in.

Dog Friendly

The Enclosed
Dog Field

Our brand new, fully enclosed dog field is a proper treat for four-legged visitors and their owners alike. Let your dog run free in a safe, secure space while you grab a coffee and enjoy the shop — no leads, no stress, just happy dogs.

A welcome new addition to Bradshaws — and a big hit with the Perton dog-walking community since day one.
